CORE Strengths
Hospitality-Grade Materials

Our fabrics are specifically engineered for hospitality use — designed to handle daily hotel operations, repeated washing, and continuous guest use without losing structure or comfort.

Consistent Quality

Every CORE product is manufactured with strict quality controls, ensuring uniform softness, size, and appearance across all rooms and future supply cycles.

Custom Sizing

We provide linen solutions tailored to your hotel’s exact bed, mattress, and pillow specifications to ensure a perfect fit and a refined guest experience.

Long-Term Durability

CORE linens are built to withstand hundreds of industrial laundry cycles while retaining strength, color, and texture — reducing replacement and operational costs.

Easy Maintenance

Our fabrics are designed for quick drying, easy ironing, and minimal shrinkage, helping housekeeping teams work faster and more efficiently.

Trusted Partnership

We don’t just supply products — we build long-term partnerships with hotels, ensuring consistent support, timely delivery, and dependable quality as your property grows.
