Our Story

CORE™ Hospitality Linen didn't start with linen.
It started with fabrics.

For over two decades, we've been manufacturing and processing fabrics in-house - long before CORE was conceived.

We've dyed, finished, tested, reworked, and perfected textiles at scale. We've seen what performs, what fails, and what quietly costs hotels money over time.

CORE was born to bring this deep textile understanding into hospitality - not as a supplier, but as a thinking partner.
