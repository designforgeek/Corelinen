Bed Accessories

Mattress & Accessories
Protection, flexibility and room styling

CORE bed accessories are designed to protect, enhance comfort and improve room presentation across high-use hospitality environments. From mattresses and toppers to protectors and soft furnishings, each program supports durability and housekeeping practicality.

Mattress Program
Pocket Spring Mattress

Crafted with high coil count steel springs for superior support, with Eurotop and Pillowtop options and fire-retardant fabrics.

Flexible Inventory
Roll Away Beds

Durable mobile extra-bed solutions with corrosion-resistant frames and a standard size of 36 x 75 x 6 inches.

Comfort Layer
Mattress Toppers

Extra comfort and protection with multiple shell, fill, skirt and thickness options for customised hospitality specifications.

Protection
Quilted Protectors

Needle or ultrasonic quilting, waterproof barriers and custom sizes designed to extend mattress and pillow life.

Hospitality Utility
Terry Laminated Protectors

Water-repellent, breathable and quick-dry constructions with zipper pillow protector options for busy room operations.

Fit Options
Skirted & Elastic Fits

Fitted and elastic-corner protector options built for easy housekeeping use and long commercial laundering life.

Protection Benefits

Waterproof Protection for mattresses and pillows in high-turnover guestrooms
Breathable Comfort-focused fabric constructions that avoid a heavy hand feel
Hypoallergenic Barrier options that support cleaner and more hygienic bedding systems
Soft Noiseless backing and comfort-led finishing for better guest acceptance
Durable Built to withstand repeated handling and commercial laundering
