PRODUCT CARE

  • Use with covers & protectors to reduce direct washing
  • Fluff & air regularly to maintain loft and freshness
  • Machine wash at 40-60°C (gentle cycle) when required
  • Use mild detergents; avoid excessive bleach
  • Tumble dry on low-medium heat; with dryer balls to avoid clumping
  • Do not compress when damp; restore shape after drying
  • Store in clean, dry & breathable conditions
