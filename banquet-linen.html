LINEN CARE

  • Pre-treat stains immediately for effective removal
  • Machine wash at 60–75°C with stain-removal detergents
  • Use mild/professional detergents; avoid excessive chlorine bleach
  • Do not overload machines to prevent wrinkling & poor finish
  • Tumble dry or flatwork iron for a crisp, professional look
  • Store flat or hung to maintain structure & drape
