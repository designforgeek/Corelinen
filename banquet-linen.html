Linen Care
  • Pre-treat stains immediately for effective removal
  • Machine wash at 60-75C with stain-removal detergents
  • Use mild or professional detergents and avoid excessive chlorine bleach
  • Do not overload machines to prevent wrinkling and poor finish
  • Tumble dry or flatwork iron for a crisp, professional look
  • Store flat or hung to maintain structure and drape
All fabrics are OEKO-TEX Standard 100 certified for safe and sustainable hospitality use.
