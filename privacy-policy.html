Overview
How CORE handles information shared with us

CORE Hospitality Linen™ ("CORE", "we", "our", or "us") values the trust of hotel owners, procurement teams, consultants, and business partners who interact with our website and sales team. This Privacy Policy explains what information we may collect, how we use it, when we may share it, and what choices you may have.

This policy applies to information collected through our website, contact inquiries, quote requests, sample requests, phone conversations, email exchanges, and related business communications. It does not apply to third-party websites or services that may be linked from our site.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not submit personal information through the website. For questions, email sanyam@corelinen.in.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact details: name, company name, job title, phone number, email address, city, and any other information you submit in an inquiry.
  • Business and procurement information: hotel or project requirements, product categories of interest, expected quantities, operational needs, customization requests, and delivery preferences.
  • Communication records: copies of emails, call notes, WhatsApp messages, contact form submissions, and follow-up correspondence with our team.
  • Technical information: IP address, browser type, device information, pages viewed, date and time of access, and referring URLs collected through normal website operation or analytics tools.

2. How We Use Information

  • To respond to product inquiries, quote requests, sample requests, and support messages.
  • To understand your business requirements and recommend appropriate hospitality linen solutions.
  • To prepare proposals, coordinate sampling, process purchase discussions, and manage ongoing business relationships.
  • To improve website performance, content quality, and user experience.
  • To maintain internal records, protect our business, prevent misuse, and comply with applicable legal obligations.
  • To send service-related communications and, where appropriate, relevant product updates or business announcements.

3. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies, server logs, and standard analytics technologies to remember preferences, understand visitor behavior, and improve functionality. These tools may collect browser and usage data, but they do not automatically identify you by name unless you separately provide that information to us.

You can usually control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain site features or performance.

4. When We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only when reasonably necessary, including:

  • With service providers or business partners who help us operate the website, communicate with customers, or support sales and logistics.
  • Within our organization for sales, support, production planning, and account management purposes.
  • When required by law, regulation, court order, or valid governmental request.
  • To protect our rights, property, safety, website integrity, or users in the event of misuse, fraud, or security concerns.
  • As part of a merger, business transfer, restructuring, or similar corporate transaction, subject to applicable obligations.

5. Retention & Security

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, maintain business records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and meet legal, tax, or compliance requirements.

We use commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information from unauthorized access, disclosure, misuse, or loss. However, no website, email system, or electronic storage method can be guaranteed to be fully secure.

6. Your Choices

  • You may request that we update or correct information you previously shared with us.
  • You may ask us to stop sending marketing or promotional communications by contacting us directly or using unsubscribe options where available.
  • Subject to applicable law, you may request access to or deletion of certain personal information we hold about you.
  • If you believe information has been used improperly, you may contact us and we will review the matter in good faith.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites, platforms, or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of those third parties. We encourage you to review their policies before sharing information with them.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website and business services are intended for business users and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information to us, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our business, website, practices, or legal requirements. Any revised version will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Continued use of the website after changes are posted will be treated as acceptance of the updated policy.
