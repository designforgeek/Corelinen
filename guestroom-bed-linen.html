LINEN CARE

  • Wash before first use
  • Machine wash at 60-75°C (commercial laundering suitable)
  • Use mild/professional detergents; avoid excessive chlorine bleach
  • Do not overload machines or over-dry to prevent shrinkage & lint
  • Tumble dry on medium heat; flatwork ironing recommended
  • Allow linen to rest 24 hrs post wash to restore fibre balance
  • Store in clean, dry & ventilated conditions
