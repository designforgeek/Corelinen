Linen Care Instructions
  • • Machine wash at 60–75°C using mild/professional detergents
  • • Wash coloured pool towels separately to avoid colour transfer
  • • Avoid mixing different towel qualities or fabrics in one cycle
  • • Avoid over-drying to maintain softness & pile integrity
  • • Do not overload washing machines or dryers
  • • Avoid excessive chlorine bleach to preserve fibres
  • • Allow towels to rest 24 hours post-wash to restore fibre balance

LINEN CARE

  • Machine wash at 60-75°C using mild/professional detergents
  • Wash coloured pool towels separately to avoid colour transfer
  • Avoid mixing different towel qualities or fabrics in one cycle
  • Avoid over-drying to maintain softness & pile integrity
  • Do not overload washing machines or dryers for effective cleaning
  • Avoid excessive chlorine bleach to preserve fibres
  • Allow towels to rest for 24 hours post-wash to restore fibre balance
